Cube Geometry Index

One numbering for faces, edges, and corners, derived entirely from the body frame. A face is its axis; an edge is the two faces it joins; a corner is the three. Nothing to memorize — read the label off the model.

X — roll · wheel B Y — pitch · wheel A Z — yaw · wheel C right-handed · rest = roll 0 / pitch 0
